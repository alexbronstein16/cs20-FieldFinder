El Camino Park

155 El Camino Real, Palo Alto, CA 94301

Field Information

  • Surface: Turf
  • Lights: Yes
  • Permanent Goals: Yes
  • Movable Goals: No
  • Amenities: Bathrooms, digital clock

El Camino Park is a public turf field located along El Camino Real in Palo Alto across from Stanford Shopping center. The park is commonly used for youth sports, casual pickup games, and club practices and games. It offers an open playing area with permanent goals and easy street access.
