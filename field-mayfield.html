Mayfield Soccer Complex

2700 El Camino Real, Palo Alto, CA 94306

Field Information

  • Surface: Turf
  • Lights: Yes
  • Permanent Goals: Yes
  • Movable Goals: Yes
  • Amenities: Bathrooms, tables, benches, digital clock

Mayfield Soccer Complex features two well-maintained turf fields located just off El Camino Real. The complex includes field lighting, permanent restrooms, a digital clock, and seating areas with tables and benches between the two fields.
