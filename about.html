About Field Finder

Field Finder helps communities make better use of public sports fields by making field availability and location information easy to access.

No longer will you have to drive field to field in order to find an open place to play. With Field Finder you can see exactly when fields are booked and when they are available for use.

Our goal is to encourage more outdoor activity by connecting people with open, accessible spaces to play.
